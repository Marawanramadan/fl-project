About Us

Vision and Purpose

We strive to be a pioneer at providing whole artistic services in the painting field with the highest quality and mastery along with our commitment to innovate and produce constructional products with the highest quality according to the latest global standards and to keep our pivotal role in the painting and insulation fields in Saudi Arabia.
Therefore we aim to be the best paint and insulation solutions company with worldclass efficency and achieve the kingdom's vision by contributing in the development of economy and the localization of the paint industry to meet the needs of customers with high-quality and environmentally friendly paints, raising the percentage of exports in global markets and encouraging the use of national products.
