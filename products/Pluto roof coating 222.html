Pluto roof coating 222

  • Pluto roof is an elastomeric acrylic roof coating. It forms a waterproofing membrane with superior weatherproofing, durability, flexibility, and color retention for long-term protection
  • pluto roof 222 is a single component basecoat / topcoat system designed for easy application
  • pluto roof system is economical, temperature reflective, and energy efficient ,used to protect, and reflect UV radiation when used over spray polyurethane foam systems, and metal roof systems
  • Excellent water resistant, by roller , Good adhesion,Elastomeric
  • Reduce temperature inside building ,Low odor ,Excellent resistance to sunlight
  • Can be used on most types of roofs, such as concrete roof, metal roof, asbestos roof, etc. As a protective membrane for Polyurethane foam installation.

Physical properties

  • Finish : silk finish
  • Color : White & black
  • Specific Gravity : 1.35 KG/Liter
  • Volume Solid : 45%
  • DFT : 250 microns
  • Spreading Rate: Theoretical spreading rate: :2.0 m2 / liter
  • Mixing Ratio : Single Pack
  • Drying Time: touch dry : 3 hours at 25 ºC , Surface Dry 2 hours at 25 ºC , Over Dry 2-3 days at 25 ºC ,Over coating Interval : 6 hours

Surfaces preparation:

  • New Surfaces :
    • All concrete, plaster, and masonry surfaces should be fully cured-minimum 28 days prior to commencing of paint application
    • In general all surfaces must be dry and free from oil, grease, dirt, efflorescence, laitance, any loose and flaking material
    • Prime with one coat of pluto roof dilution 30% by sweet water.
    • Fill small cracks and surface imperfection with interior/exterior pluto sp wall filler, large cracks to be filled with acrylic sealant material.
  • Old Surfaces:
    • All surfaces should be sound, clean, dry and free from loose and flaking material, chalking, dirt, oil, grease, fungus, moulds, moss, wax and polish.
    • Apply one coat pluto roof dilution 30% by sweet water . Where necessary fill small cracks and voids with pluto sp wall filler, large cracks may be filled with acrylic sealant.
  • Coating system :
    • A primer coat is required to seal the pores and stabilize the surface
    • pluto roof diluted with 30% sweet water may be used as a primer coat. It will improve the bond strength of the coating.
    • The primer coat should be allowed to dry completely before the application of pluto roof The drying time would be a minimum of 6 hours at 25° C.
    • Apply one coat dry film thickness 125 micron.
    • Apply next coat after full dry first coat

Application methods

  • Roller
  • Spray Gun
  • Brush

Safety For Use:

  • Use only in well ventilated areas.
  • In case of insufficient ventilation during application wear suitable respiratory equipment.
  • Do not breathe vapour/spray. In case of contact with eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of sweet water and seek medical advice
