Pluto Aluminum Paint 1111/602

  • Pluto Aluminum Paint is based on resin-oil combination. It is used for wide range of industrial application such as steel structures.
  • Metallic finish , Excellent rust rasistance ,good protective property for steel
  • Temperature resistance, Excellent hiding and easy application , Single component.
  • Primarily used for wide range of industrial application such as steel structures.

Physical properties

  • Finish : Bright metallic
  • Color : Silver grey
  • Viscosity : 55KU at 30oC.
  • Volume solid: 40%,
  • Spreading Rate: Theoretical spreading rate: 16m2/L at .30u DFT.
  • Drying Time:Dry to Touch 1 hour,Surface Dry 3 hours ,Full Dry 10 hours , Recoatability 10 hours

Surfaces preparation:

  • All the surface must be free of oil, grease, moisture, dirt,. rust or any other form of contaminants
  • Apply two coats of " Pluto Aluminum Paint"

Application methods

  • Spray Gun : Recommended Nozzle size : (1.5-1.8)mm
  • Roller : General / Normal typ
  • Brush: General / Normal type

Safety For Use:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight with good ventilation.
  • Keep out of reach of children and direct flame.
  • Avoid eye contact and skin for a long time, if it happened, wash thoroughly with fresh water immediately
