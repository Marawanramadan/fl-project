Pluto epoxy clear coat

  • A two-component, high gloss clear lacquer based on polyamide cured epoxy resin.
  • Recommended to use as a clear coat over steel, concrete, top coat epoxy floor, tiles and brick surfaces for interior applications
  • Fully transparent, High gloss , Excellent scratch resistance ,Excellent resistance to chemicals and solvents , Excellent water resistance.

Physical properties

  • Finish : Gloss
  • Color : Clear
  • Viscosity : 50 KU
  • Volume Solid: 100 %
  • Theoretical Spreading Rate: 9 m2/L at D.F.T. 50 μ.

Surfaces preparation:

  • New surfaces :
    • All new surfaces must be cured at least for 30 days prior to application of the primer.
    • The surface must be treated by removing all forms release and protrusions to get a suitable surface for application.
  • For previously painted surfaces:
    • Remove old paint from surfaces completely.
    • Make sure that the surface is clean , dry , free from dust, oil, grease, salt and mold.
    • Fill the holes and cracks with suitable fillers pluto ready putty
  • The surface must be removed from dust , grease, oil and .other contaminants completely
  • Surface temperature required during application must not be less than 10°C; the surface moisture should not exceed 8% and the relative humidity at work place not to exceed 70%.
  • Maximum pH of 9.
  • Make sure that, base paint and hardener are mixed uniformly.
  • A suitable quantity of “pluto thenner 119” can be added to get reasonable viscosity for application.
  • The mixed paint should be used within potlife
  • Do not apply on wet and moistened surfaces until get completely dry.
  • Do not apply the next coat unless the previous one is fully dry. Minimum recoating interval time should be given between the two coats
  • Adequate ventilation should be provided during drying time.

Application methods

  • Spray Gun : Thinning ratio: (15– 20)% by volume. Nozzle size: (1.5 – 1.8) mm.
  • Roller : Thinning ratio: (5 – 10) % by volume.
  • Brush: Thinning ratio: (5 – 10) % by volume.

Safety For Use:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight with good ventilation.
  • Keep out of reach of children and direct flame.
  • Avoid eye contact and skin for a long time, if it happened, wash thoroughly with fresh water immediately
