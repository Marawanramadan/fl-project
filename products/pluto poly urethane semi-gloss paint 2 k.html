pluto poly urethane semi-gloss paint 2 k

  • top coat semi gloss , high coverage rate , excellent resistance to weather changes ,high adhesion to the surface , durable , quick drying , excellent impact and scratch resistance
  • semigloss poly urethane with excellent adhesion.
  • High resistance to household detergents ,High scratch and abrasion resistance when fully dry
  • Good gloss retention and UV resistance ,Flame spread resistance
  • suitable for interior and exterior use. It can be used for most kind of wooden substrate and floor paint and steal and steel tank .

Physical properties

  • Finish : semi gloss finish
  • Color : as pluto catalogue.
  • Specific Gravity : 1.2
  • Viscosity :70KU
  • Volume Solid :60%
  • Theoretical spreading rate: 9-11m2 /L at .40-45u DFT
  • Paint: hardener ratio: 4: 1
  • Drying Time: Surface Dry 2 hours , Hard dry 10 hours ,Second coat apply 10 hours

Surfaces preparation:

  • The surfaces should be dry, clean and free from contaminates such as grease, oil, dust, which can result in poor adhesion of the paint to the surfaces.
  • Use a suitable solvent for cleaning, old flaking paint or poorly bound surfaces must be removed completely
  • Mixing materials before usin
  • Used thinner silver 1300 for dilution.

Application methods

  • Roller
  • Spray Gun
  • Brush

Safety For Use:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight with good ventilation
  • Keep out of reach of children and direct flame.
  • Avoid eye contact and skin for a long time, if it happened, wash thoroughly with fresh water immediately
