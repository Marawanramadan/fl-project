Pluto epoxy floor paint

  • Pluto epoxy floor is a high performance high build two component chemical resistant multipurpose coating based on modified cured epoxy resins floor coating.
  • The product dries to form a smooth, though, durable finish that has outstanding resistance to solvents, oil, gasoline, jet fuel, diesel and dilute acids and alkalis
  • can used for a wide range of floors as industrial floors, laboratories, hospitals, warehouses, factories ,auto/truck repair and hangars.

Physical properties

  • Gloss : semi gloss
  • Color : gray,white, red , blue, yellow, green
  • Volume Solid: 100 %
  • Water resistance: Very good
  • Abrasion resistance: Very good
  • Solvent resistance : Excellent
  • Chemical resistance : Excellent
  • Flexibility: Good
  • Spreading Rate:
    • Dry film thickness in spreading rate microns (μm) : 50 , 80 , 100
    • Theoretical spreading rate (m2/l) : 10.5 ,6.5 ,5

Surfaces preparation:

  • To achieve good adhesion All surfaces should be clean, dry and free from contamination.
  • The temperature of the substrate should be minimum 10°C and at least 3°C above the dew point of the air
  • temperature and relative humidity measured in the vicinity of the substrate.
  • Good ventilation is usually required in confined areas to ensure proper drying.

Application methods

  • Spray Gun : Use airless spray.
  • Roller: May be used. Care must be taken to achieve the specified dry film thickness.
  • Brush: Recommended for stripe coating and small areas. Care must be taken to achieve the specified dry film thickness.

Safety For Use:

  • Please observe the precautionary notices displayed on the container. Use under well ventilated conditions.
  • Do not inhale spray mist. Avoid skin contact. Spillage on the skin should immediately be removed with suitable cleanser, soap and water.
  • Eyes should be well flushed with water and medical attention sought immediately.
