ASB ROOF COATING 222

  • ASB roof is an elastomeric acrylic roof coating.
  • It forms a waterproofing membrane with superior weatherproofing, durability, flexibility, and color retention for long-term protection.
  • ASB roof 222 is a single component basecoat / topcoat system designed for easy application.
  • economical, temperature reflective, and energy efficient.
  • used to protect, and reflect UV radiation when used over spray polyurethane foam systems, and metal roof systems.

Physical properties

  • Color : White & black
  • Volume solid : 45
  • Surface dry : 2 hours at 25 ºC
  • Touch dry : 3 hours at 25 ºC
  • Hard dry : 2-3 days at 25 ºC

Surface preparation

  • New Surfaces: All concrete, plaster, and masonry surfaces should be fully cured-minimum 28 days prior to commencing of paint application. In general all surfaces must be dry and free from oil, grease, dirt, efflorescence, laitance, any loose and flaking material. Prime with one coat of ASB roof dilution 30% by sweet water. Fill small cracks and surface imperfection with interior/exterior ASB sp wall filler, large cracks to be filled with acrylic sealant material.
  • Old Surfaces: All surfaces should be sound, clean, dry and free from loose and flaking material, chalking, dirt, oil, grease, fungus, moulds, moss, wax and polish. Apply one coat ASB roof dilution 30% by sweet water . Where necessary fill small cracks and voids with ASB sp wall filler, large cracks may be filled with acrylic sealant.

Application method

  • Air spray
  • Roller
  • Brush

Health and Safety

  • Use only in well ventilated areas. In case of insufficient ventilation during application wear suitable respiratory equipment
  • Do not breathe vapour/spray. In case of contact with eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of sweet water and seek medical advice
