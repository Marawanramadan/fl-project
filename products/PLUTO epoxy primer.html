PLUTO epoxy primer

  • Pluto epoxy Primer is a two-pack epoxy primer based on a high molecular weight epoxy resin.
  • Anticorrosive primer Can be used on steel, galvanized steel or Aluminum and concrete

Physical properties

  • Gloss : Flat
  • Flash point 25ºC ± 2
  • Color : gray
  • Solids (vol %)* 51 ± 2
  • Water resistance: Very good
  • Abrasion resistance: Very good
  • Solvent resistance : Excellent
  • Chemical resistance : Excellent
  • Flexibility: Good
  • Drying Time: Drying times are generally related to air circulation, temperature, film thickness and number of coats, and will be affected correspondingly. The figures given are typical with:
    • * Good ventilation (Outdoor exposure or free circulation of air)
    • Typical film thickness
    • One coat on top of inert substrate
  • Surface dry : 2 h at 10°C , 1 h at 23°C , 0,5 h at 40°C
  • Through dry :14 h at 10°C , 6,5 h at 23°C ,3 h at 40°C
  • Cured :14 d at 10°C ,7 d at 23°C ,3 d at 40°C
  • Dry to recoat :8 h at 10°C , 4 h at 23°C ,3 h at 40°C

Surfaces preparation:

  • All surfaces should be clean, dry and free from contamination. The surface should be assessed and treated
  • Bare steel
    • Cleanliness: Blast cleaning to Sa 2½ (ISO 8501-1:1988)
    • Roughness: using abrasives suitable to achieve grade Fine to Medium G (30-85 μm, Ry5)
  • The temperature of the substrate should be minimum 10°C and at least 3°C above the dew point of the air, temperature and relative humidity measured in the vicinity of the substrate. The coating should not be exposed to oil, chemicals or mechanical stress until cured.
  • Good ventilation is usually required in confined areas to ensure proper drying.
  • The coating should not be exposed to oil, chemicals or mechanical stress until cured.

Application methods

  • Spray Gun : Use airless spray, air spray
  • Brush : Recommended for stripe coating and small areas, care must be taken to achieve the specified dry film thickness.

Safety For Use:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight with good ventilation
  • Keep out of reach of children and direct flame.
  • Avoid eye contact and skin for a long time, if it happened, wash thoroughly with fresh water immediately
