PLUTO medium texture

  • An attractive, high build spray applied textured coating based on acrylic polymer containing crushed marble for long term protection and durability
  • For exterior decoration and protection ,Highly durable
  • Very good weather resistance ,Very good resistance against cleaning
  • Can be formed in different rough texture designs, by roller , spray gun
  • Highly durable ,Easy to apply
  • Suitable for exterior and interior use on a wide variety of substrates including masonry , concrete, gypsum board, block work, plaster, and brick work

Physical properties

  • Finish : Matt, Rough Texture
  • Color : white or as per pluto texture Color Catalog
  • Specific Gravity : 1.6
  • Viscosity : High viscous
  • Volume Solid : 45%
  • coverage : ( 1.2 – 1.8) m2/Kg . Depending on the : design, thickness Of texture, nature of the surface and application condition
  • Drying Time: Surface Dry 2 hours , Recoat Interval 24 hours

Surfaces preparation:

  • the surface must be clean and ready to paint
  • Apply one coat of interior sealer for interior surfaces or exterior sealer for exterior surfaces and leave for full dry.
  • Apply one coat of pluto Texture using spray gun or roller.

Application methods

  • Roller : Recommended type
  • Spray Gun :Recommended Nozzle size : (5.5 – 8.0)mm
  • Sponge

Safety For Use:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight with good ventilation
  • Keep out of reach of children and direct flame.
  • Avoid eye contact and skin for a long time, if it happened, wash thoroughly with fresh water immediately
