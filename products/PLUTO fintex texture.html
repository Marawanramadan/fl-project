PLUTO fintex texture

  • An attractive textured based on acrylic polymer containing fine marble
  • For interior, exterior decoration , Have fine design and attractive shape
  • Good weather resistance ,Very good resistance against cleaning
  • Excellent adhesion ,Highly durable
  • Suitable for interior and exterior use on a wide variety of substrates including masonry , concrete, gypsum board, block work, plaster, and brick work

Physical properties

  • Finish : Matt, fine Texture
  • Color : Available in white color and a wide range of pluto colors
  • Viscosity : High viscous
  • Volume Solid : 45%
  • Spreading Rate : ( 1.2 – 1.8 ) m2/Kg . Depending on the : design, thickness Of texture, nature of the surface and application condition
  • Drying Time: Surface Dry 2 hours , Full Dry 24 hours

Surfaces preparation:

  • Clean the surface and prepare it properly to be ready for paint.
  • Apply one coat of interior or exterior sealer pluto depending on surface and leave for full dry
  • Apply one coat of Pluto finetex Texture using spray gun or roller.
  • Instructions:
    • Paint should be stirred carefully and homogenously before use.
    • A suitable quantity of Water can be added to get reasonable Viscosity for application
    • Dust , grease, oil and other dirties must be cleaned and removed completely from the surface
    • previous paints must be removed completely from the surface And the surface should be prepared well.
    • Do not apply on wet and moistened surfaces until get completely dry
    • Do not apply the next coat unless the previous one is fully dry minimum recoating interval time should be given between the two coats.
    • Surface temperature required during application must not be less than 10ْ c .

Application methods

  • Spray Gun : Recommended Nozzle size : (5 – 6)mm Small plastic roller for pressing

Safety For Use:

  • Must be careful contact of liquid paint and thinner with eyes and skin
  • Inhalation of paint mist and solvent vapor , should be strongly avoided
