PLUTO PROFILE

  • Texture paint , durable and continuous plastic coating based on acrylic polymer resin .applied by trowel to give a smoothed , coarse finish with a broken scratch work effect
  • High resistance to moisture, temperature and weather conditions.
  • High resistance to scratch and friction ,Easy to apply and formation in different shapes
  • : Suitable for exterior and interior use on a wide variety of substrates including masonry , concrete, gypsum board, block work, plaster, and brick work

Physical properties

  • Finish : Matt with a smooth coarse texture
  • Color : pluto profile texture Color Catalog
  • Spreading Rate: 1 drum speading approximately 11 meter . Depending on the design, thickness of the coat, nature of the surface and application conditions.
  • Drying Time: Surface Dry 1 hours , Full Dry 24 hours

Surfaces preparation:

  • The surface must be clean and free from dirt, oilgreases, and oldpaints. - by using wire brush or scraper.
  • Removing all this materials then clean surface by water, repair the surface by pluto ready putty and allow to completely dry
  • Apply on from pluto exterior sealer plus.
  • Apply one coat of " pluto profile " by steal trowel
  • Make the required design by the rough plastic trowel; clean the trowel from time to time during application with a sponge saturated with tap water.

Application methods

  • Steel Trowel
  • Plastic Trowel

Safety For Use:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight with good ventilation
  • Keep out of reach of children and direct flame.
  • Avoid eye contact and skin for a long time, if it happened, wash thoroughly with fresh water immediately
