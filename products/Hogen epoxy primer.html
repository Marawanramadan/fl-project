Hogen epoxy primer

  • Hogen epoxy Primer is a two-pack epoxy primer based on a high molecular weight epoxy resin.
  • Anticorrosive primer Can be used on steel, galvanized steel or Aluminum and concrete and floor primer .
  • Film thickness, dry (μm) Minimum 40 ,Maximum 60,Typical 50
  • Film thickness, wet (μm) Minimum 80 ,Maximum 120 ,Typical 100
  • Theoretical spreading rate (m²/l) Minimum 12.8 ,Maximum 8.5 ,Typical 10.2

Physical properties

  • Solids (vol %)* 51 ± 2
  • Color gray
  • Water resistance: Very good
  • Abrasion resistance: Very good
  • Solvent resistance : Excellent
  • Chemical resistance : Excellent
  • Flexibility: Good

Surfaces preparation:

  • All surfaces should be clean, dry and free from contamination. The surface should be assessed and treated
  • Bare steel Cleanliness: Blast cleaning to Sa 2½ (ISO 8501-1:1988).Roughness: using abrasives suitable to achieve grade Fine to Medium G (30-85 μm, Ry5)

Application methods

  • Spray: Use airless spray, air spray
  • Brush: Recommended for stripe coating and small areas, care must be taken to achieve the specified dry film thickness.

Safety For Use:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight with good ventilation
  • Keep out of reach of children and direct flame.
  • Avoid eye contact and skin for a long time, if it happened, wash thoroughly with fresh water immediately
