pluto acrylic clear coat 602

  • No smell, environmentally friendly, water based transparent varnish based on modified acrylic emulsion
  • Recommended for interior and exterior application where high quality finish is required
  • Good water, alkali and salt resistance ,Flexible to accommodate hair cracks in substrate.
  • Yellowing property Excellent non ,Washable with water and soap
  • Recommended for application over matt emulsion paints, masonry, bricks, plaster, concrete and cements sheets

Physical properties

  • Finish : Glossy
  • Color : clear
  • Viscosity : 20 KU
  • Solid content : 27%
  • Spreading Rate : Theoretical spreading rate: 16m 2/L at D.F.T. 30
  • Drying Time: Surface Dry 1 hours , Full Dry 4 hours ,Recoatability 8 hours

Surfaces preparation:

  • the surfaces should be dry, clean and free from contaminates such as grease, oil, dust, which can result in poor adhesion of the paint to the surfaces.
  • Use a suitable solvent for cleaning, old flaking paint or poorly bound surfaces must be removed completely
  • For Exterior Surfaces :
    • Apply one coat of pluto exterior sealer or pluto interior sealer and must be full dry
    • Apply two coats of pluto acrylic Gloss with deference drying time for each coat.
  • For Interior Surfaces :
    • Apply one coat of pluto interior sealer.
    • Apply two coats of pluto Ready putty the surface must be clean and smooth.
    • Apply two coats of pluto Acrylic Gloss Acrylic with deference drying time.

Application methods

  • Spray Gun
  • Brush
  • Roller

Safety For Use:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight with good ventilation
  • Keep out of reach of children and direct flame.
  • Avoid eye contact and skin for a long time, if it happened, wash thoroughly with fresh water immediately
