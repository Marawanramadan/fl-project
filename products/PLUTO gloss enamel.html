PLUTO gloss enamel

  • A durable, alkyd based, high gloss finish which combines excellent opacity and light fastness, with resistance to yellowing and chalking
  • High gloss ,Excellent covering and hiding property
  • Very good weather resistance property ,Washable with water and soap
  • suitable for interior and exterior use on most of substrates including metal, wood, concrete, gypsum board, plaster, plastic and building boards .

Physical properties

  • Finish : high Gloss
  • Gloss : Above 85, at angle of 60 degrees
  • Specific Gravity : 1.21
  • Color : Available in white color and a wide rang of pluto colors
  • Viscosity : 105 KU
  • Volume Solid :43+-1
  • Spreading Rate : 18m 2/L at W.F.T. 30u
  • Drying Time: Dry to Touch 2 hour Surface Dry 3 hours , Full Dry 16 hours ,Recoatability 24 hours

Surfaces preparation:

  • the surfaces should be dry, clean and free from contaminates such as grease, oil, dust, which can result in poor adhesion of the paint to the surfaces.
  • Use a suitable solvent for cleaning, old flaking paint or poorly bound surfaces must be removed completely
  • For building surfaces :
    • Apply one coat of pluto interior sealer.
    • Apply two coats of pluto Ready putty the surface must be clean and smooth.
    • Apply two coats of Pluto high gloss enamel with deference.
  • For Steel surfaces :
    • Prepare the surface by sanding and blasting process until all rust, oil and grease are fully removed .
    • Clean the surface by soft brush or cloth with Pluto Thinner 108 and Allow to full dry.
    • Apply one or two coats of Pluto Under coat or Pluto Red Oxide and allow to full dry
    • Apply two coats of Pluto high Gloss enamel If required , giving sufficient recoat interval time between two coats.

Application methods

  • Spray Gun :Recommended Nozzle size : (1.5-1.8)mm
  • Brush : suitable/ good type
  • Roller :Recommended type Soft/Smooth

Safety For Use:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight with good ventilation
  • Keep out of reach of children and direct flame.
  • Avoid eye contact and skin for a long time, if it happened, wash thoroughly with fresh water immediately
