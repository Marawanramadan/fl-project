PLUTO acrylic INTERIOR EMULSION

  • PLUTO interior emulsion water thinable ,high opacity, matt , smooth finish
  • Smooth surface with matt finishes ,Excellent covering and hiding property
  • Good water, alkali , salt resistance , Excellent adhesion property.
  • suitable for the decoration of interior walls and ceilings , applied in to new plaster, cement, concrete, gypsum board

Physical properties

  • Finish : matt
  • Color : Produced in white & other colors as per PLUTO Color Catalog
  • Viscosity : 120 KU at 25ْ c
  • Volume Solid : 43%
  • Spreading Rate : Theoretical spreading rate: 13 2/L at W.F.T. 40u
  • Drying Time: Surface Dry 30 minutes, Full Dry 2 hours ,Recoat Interval (Minimum) 4 hours

Surfaces preparation:

  • Clean the surface and prepare it properly to be ready for paint
  • Completely remove the old paint from the surface if any - and make sure that the surface completely clean and free of dirt and dust and dirt
  • Apply one coat of PLUTO interior sealer .
  • Filling all cracks by PLUTO ready putty with sandbility.
  • Apply two coats of PLUTO interior emulsion anti bacteria.

Application methods

  • Spray Gun : Recommended Nozzle size : (1.5-1.8)mm
  • Roller Recommended type Soft/Smooth woolen roller
  • Brush suitable/ good type
